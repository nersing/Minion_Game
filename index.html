Minions Needed for a Mission

Help Gru gather as many minions as he needs for his mission.

You will be given a random number of minions to collect at the start of the game.

There are four minions below. By clicking on a minion, you will add a specific amount of minions to your total minion count.

You will win the game by matching your total minion count to the amount of minions Gru needs for his mission.

The value of each minion is hidden from you until you click on it.

BEWARE!! Everytime the game starts, the value of each minion will change.
